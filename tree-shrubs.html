Seasonal Guide

The Right Care at the Right Time

Trees and shrubs have specific seasonal needs — we know exactly when to act.

▲

Spring

Fertilize, apply pre-emergent to beds, prune spring-blooming shrubs after they flower.

☀

Summer

Deep-water during drought, monitor for pest pressure, light shaping as needed.

▼

Fall

Plant new trees and shrubs, apply winter mulch, cut back ornamental grasses.

❄

Winter

Structural pruning of dormant trees, spray dormant oil for overwintering pest eggs.
